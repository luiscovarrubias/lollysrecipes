Banana Smoothie Recipe

This is Luis's Famous Banana Smoothie recipe, meaning I found this online when trying to find a way to use the 20 something bananas mom had at home that were all turning very brown. Tastes very good, good for 2 servings, or a maniac like me that has drank it straight out the blender glass with a straw, don't recommend.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla
  • 2 bananas
  • 2 cups ice


Instructions

Step 1

Place all ingredients in the blender and blend for several minutes until smooth.
