Shrimp Recipe

This recipe is for 1 lb of shrimp, but can go up depending on how many lbs you make. For every lb of shrimp, use 2 sticks of butter (Ie: 2lbs of shrimp = 4 sticks). Also this is what leaves the "juice" when you finish the shrimp, so the more "juice" you want, the more sticks of butter you should add.

Ingredients

  • Tony Chachere Original Creole Mix
  • 16 Tbsp of butter (2 sticks)
  • 1 lb of shrimp


Instructions

Step 1

Slow cook the shrimp on low heat

Step 2

Add the sticks of butter as you cook them, don't have to wait for anything to add them

Step 3

Once the shrimp starts to turn red, add seasoning (amount depends on how much you can handle, taste as you add situation)

Step 4

After adding seasoning, make sure you mix it in then you're done.
