Caldo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef ribs
  • 2 lbs beef shank
  • 2 lbs beef roast
  • 1 onion
  • 1 garlic head
  • 5 or 5 ½ Qt. of water
  • 4 ½ Tbsp
  • 1 Tbsp beef bouillon
  • 1 Tbsp tomato bouillon

    • Vegetables

  • 4-5 corn on the cob
  • 4-5 golden potatoes
  • 5-6 carrots or 1 lb baby carrots
  • ½ lb green beans
  • 2 chayotes
  • 2 mexican squash
  • 1 bunch of cilantro
  • 1 cabbage head
  • 1 serving of love

Prep

Need a chopping board a huge pot to put everything in and cook.


Instructions

Step 1

Chop every ingredient up and put into pot.

Step 2

After removing scum (impurities from meat) reduce heat to medium/low. This is very important so you don't overcook the vegetables
