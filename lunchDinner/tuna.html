Tuna Recipe

The ingredients list looks more like a grocery list but that's because it is completely dependent on how much of each ingredient you want. If you like/dislike onion or celery or anything else, you can add a lot or a small amount depending on how you want it.

Ingredients

  • Starkist Canned Tuna (In Water, very important)
  • Miracle Whip
  • Mustard (for color)
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Eggs
  • Celery
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Prep

Just need one bowl and a chopping board.


Instructions

Only Step

Chop every ingredient up chunky or thin depending on your preference and mix it all together in one big bowl, and that's it.
