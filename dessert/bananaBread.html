Banana Bread Recipe

This is actually one of my favorite pieces of food of everything on here, it comes out very moist and makes for a good, small breakfast bite.

Ingredients

  • 3 very ripe bananas
  • ½ cup unsalted butter room temperature (8 Tbsp)
  • ¾ cup granluated sugar
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 ½ all-purpose flour
  • 1 Tsp baking soda
  • ½ Tsp salt
  • ½ Tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup walnuts
  • ½ cup raisins

Prep

Preheat the oven to 350°F. Grease and flour a bread loaf pan.


Instructions

Step 1

Lightly roast walnuts on a skillet, continuously stirring so they won't burn. Coarsely chop and cool to room temperature.

Step 2

In a mixing bowl, cream together 8 Tbsp softened butter and ¾ cup sugar.

Step 3

Mash bananas with a fork until the consistency of chunky applesauce and add them to the batter along with 2 eggs, mixing until blended.

Step 4

In a seperate bowl, whisk together 1 ½ cups of flour, 1 Tsp of baking soda and ½ Tsp of salt then add to batter.

Step 5

Add ½ Tsp of vanilla extract and mix in chopped walnuts and raisins. Pour into prepared loaf pan.

Step 6

Bake in preheated oven for 55-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Step 7

Let banana bread rest for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool.
