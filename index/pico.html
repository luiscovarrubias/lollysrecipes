Pico De Gallo Recipe

The ingredients list is completely dependent on how much of each ingredient you want. If you want it really hot, add lots of pepper and not a lot of tomato. If you want it more tame, add lots of tomato and less pepper. If you want a good kick but not super hot, add a similar amount of pepper and tomato and keep adding tomato until desired taste is achieved.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Serrano Peppers
  • White/Purple Onion
  • Avocado
  • Cilantro
  • Lemon
  • Salt

Prep

Just need one bowl and a chopping board.


Instructions

Only Step

Chop every ingredient up and put in a bowl. Ready to serve with tortilla chips or put each with a main course
