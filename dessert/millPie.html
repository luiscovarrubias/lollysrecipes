Millionaire Pie Recipe

This recipe is for the famous Millionaire Pie, one thing to note before making is that this has to be made in one day's advance. Part of the recipe is leaving the finished pie in the fridge over night so with that in mind let's get to it!

Ingredients

  • Graham cracker crust
  • Large container of Cool Whip
  • 1 pkg Philadelphia Cream Cheese
  • Pecan pieces
  • Crushed pinepple (Small Can)
  • 1 cup of Sugar

Prep

Thaw the Philadelphia Cream Cheese


Instructions

Step 1

Whip the cream cheese for about 1 minute with a fork

Step 2

Add sugar (mix for about 1 minute)

Step 3

Add the crushed pineapple (mix for about 1 minute)

Step 4

Add about 1 ½ cups of pecan pieces (mix for about 1 minute)

Step 5

Add the tub of Cool Whip (mix for about 3 minutes)

Step 6

Pour into the pie crust

Step 7

Sprinkle more pecan pieces on top of pie (as much as you like)

Step 8

Place in refrigerator over night

Step 9

Enjoy!
